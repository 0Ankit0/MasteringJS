Audio MIME Types

Back to Overview

1. audio/mpeg

What it represents: MP3 audio files.

Use case: Commonly used for music and other audio content. 

Content-Type: audio/mpeg

Example:

Content-Type: audio/mpeg

2. audio/wav

What it represents: WAV audio files.

Use case: Used for high-quality audio recordings.

Content-Type: audio/wav

Example:

Content-Type: audio/wav

3. audio/ogg

What it represents: OGG audio files.

Use case: Used for streaming and storing audio data. 

Content-Type: audio/ogg

Example:

Content-Type: audio/ogg

4. audio/aac

What it represents: AAC (Advanced Audio Coding) audio files.

Use case: Used for lossy digital audio compression. 

Content-Type: audio/aac

Example:

Content-Type: audio/aac

5. audio/webm

What it represents: WebM audio files.

Use case: Used for audio content in the WebM format. 

Content-Type: audio/webm

Example:

Content-Type: audio/webm
