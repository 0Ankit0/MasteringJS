Video MIME Types

Back to Overview

1. video/mp4

What it represents: MP4 video format.

Use case: Commonly used for video streaming and playback on the web. 

Content-Type: video/mp4

Example:

<video controls><source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4"></video>

2. video/webm

What it represents: WebM video format, an open-source alternative for video streaming.

Use case: Often used for HTML5 video playback on the web. 

Content-Type: video/webm

Example:

<video controls><source src="video.webm" type="video/webm"></video>

3. video/ogg

What it represents: OGG video format.

Use case: Used for streaming and storing video content. 

Content-Type: video/ogg

Example:

<video controls><source src="video.ogv" type="video/ogg"></video>

4. video/avi

What it represents: AVI (Audio Video Interleave) video format.

Use case: Used for storing video and audio data in a single file. 

Content-Type: video/avi

Example:

Content-Type: video/avi

5. video/mpeg

What it represents: MPEG video format.

Use case: Used for digital television and DVD video. 

Content-Type: video/mpeg

Example:

Content-Type: video/mpeg
